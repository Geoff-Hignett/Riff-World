About

Riff World is a guitar tuition service which caters for all styles and teaches in person, online, individually and in groups. Founded in 2018 by two metalheads in the home counties, we have grown from strength to strength and are passionate about getting people of all ages into music.

We Are Riff World

Services

Click on one of the pictures below to find out about what we have to offer.

Private Tuition
Private Tuitionclose

Led by Arjun Flora, our team of guitar teachers cover every style of guitar playing from metal to country. We make sure that all instructors are members of the Registry of Guitar Tutors (London College of Music) and have at least two years experience in tuition. Unbelievably we manage to do all this and keep our prices competitive.

Tab Service
Tab Serviceclose

Are you tired of watching YouTube videos where the person who is claiming to be teaching you is just showing off? Have no fear! Riff World create bespoke guitar tabs for specific song requests. We also provide the option of an instructional video. We promise to refund anyone who finds any of our tabs to be off by a single note!

Group Events
Group Eventsclose

Every month there is at least one Riff World group event. These are often jams organised in community centres and colleges by our Head of Events and metalhead Pep Marcantuono. Our biggest annual event is our Summer School which attracts over 200 guitarists! This year's event looks set to be a record-breaker for attendance.
